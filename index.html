The person behind Ruhanvi

I had everything I was supposed to want.
It took me years to admit none of it was mine.

"I did not leave corporate life. I found myself."

— Priyanka Dutta, Founder

The Beginning

I walked out of one of India's most prestigious institutions with a degree from IIM Kozhikode — and into a life that looked perfect on paper.

Corner office. Corporate title. A salary that made people nod approvingly at dinner parties. From the outside, I had made it. From the inside, I was slowly disappearing.

"Every morning I performed the role perfectly. I was very good at it. That was actually the problem."

Where it actually began

Before IIM, before the career, before all of it — there was my mother. She was a mineralogist with the Geological Survey of India. One of the few women in a field of men. She had hands that could read a stone the way most people read a face.

I grew up in a home full of minerals, charts and long quiet conversations about what the earth holds and what it heals. She taught me that stones carry measurable frequency. That planetary cycles exert real influence on human energy — the way the moon moves the ocean, something that large and that close does not leave us unchanged. She taught me to read that influence the way her colleagues read geological formations. Precisely. Systematically. Not as faith. As observed pattern.

"She used to say — most people spend their whole life being impressive to people who do not matter. Choose to be useful to people who are struggling. That is the rarer thing."

The decision

Leaving was terrifying. I had no salary lined up, no plan, no safety net. Just a management education, everything my mother had taught me, and the kind of certainty that makes you act even when you are scared.

I built Ruhanvi because I was tired of a wellness industry that sells feeling without substance. What I offer is different — it is rigour applied to energy. The same methodical precision my mother brought to mineralogy, applied to understanding what a specific person needs at a specific moment in their life.

Why You Can Trust This

I am not a hobbyist who discovered crystals on Instagram. I am a woman who grew up holding them — who learned their properties from a geologist, their energetic properties from years of deep study, and their business from one of India's finest management institutions.

When I recommend a crystal for you, I am drawing on three generations of knowledge. When I read your chart, I am speaking a language my family has spoken for decades. That is what makes Ruhanvi different. And that is why it works.
