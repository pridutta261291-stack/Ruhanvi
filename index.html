The Soul Behind Ruhanvi

Why I left everything.
And why I never looked back.

🌸

"I did not leave corporate life. I found myself."

— Priyanka Dutta, Founder

The Beginning

I walked out of one of India's most prestigious institutions with a degree from IIM Kozhikode — and into a life that looked perfect on paper.

Corner office. Corporate title. A salary that made people nod approvingly at dinner parties. From the outside, I had made it. From the inside, I was slowly disappearing.

"Every morning I would sit at my desk and feel this quiet grief — like I was living someone else's story. A very well-written story. But not mine."

Where It All Began

Long before IIM, long before spreadsheets and quarterly targets — there was my mother. She spent her career as a mineralogist with the Geological Survey of India, one of the few women in her field, with hands that could read a stone the way others read a book.

Growing up, our home was filled with crystals, charts, and quiet conversations about the earth — what it holds, what it heals, what it remembers. She taught me that every stone carries a frequency. That the universe communicates through matter. And alongside this, she taught me Vedic astrology — not as superstition, but as a precise language of planetary energy that has guided civilisations for thousands of years.

"She would say — beta, you can have a title or you can have a purpose. Very few people are brave enough to choose the second one."

The Decision

The day I left corporate life was terrifying. No salary. No title. Just a woman with an IIM degree, a deep knowledge of crystals inherited from a scientist mother, and a stubborn belief that healing is not alternative — it is essential.

I started Ruhanvi because I wanted to build something that was both scientifically grounded and spiritually alive. Every crystal I sell has been chosen with the same rigour my mother brought to mineralogy. Every reading I give carries the same precision she brought to geology.

Why You Can Trust This

I am not a hobbyist who discovered crystals on Instagram. I am a woman who grew up holding them — who learned their properties from a geologist, their energies from a Vedic astrologer, and their business from one of India's finest management institutions.

When I recommend a crystal for you, I am drawing on three generations of knowledge. When I read your chart, I am speaking a language my family has spoken for decades. This is not a business. This is my dharma.
